DOORS

Luxury Doors for your Elegant home

Our aluminium doors are the essence of low maintenance, embodying sleek modernity in every line. These doors are more than just entryways; they're custom-crafted statements of luxury and elegance tailored to your home's needs. We offer a range of Solid doors and aluminium sliding doors with glass that not only elevate your home's aesthetic but also come packed with features like noise reduction, thermal insulation, water sealing, and resistance to harsh winds. .

We know how vital ease of maintenance is for you, so we've designed our doors to be hassle-free when it comes to cleaning. Plus, they're made to stand up to Indian weather conditions. You can also personalize your space with a variety of colours and finishes to choose from.

Our door solutions include a wide array of styles such as aluminium sliding doors for smooth transitions, glass doors that make a statement, folding doors for flexible space management, and robust solid entrance doors that welcome you home. We also specialize in designing doors for specific rooms, like pooja rooms, bedrooms, and kitchens, ensuring each one fits its purpose perfectly.

Choose us for doors that blend functionality with elegance, making your home a true reflection of your style.

Ultra-Slim Sliding

This ultra-slim sliding door has a profile wall thickness of just 28 mm and is also available as TGU or DGU bullet proof glass which provides added thermal insulation. The large glass panels provide clear and unobstructed views of your surroundings. The doors slide with minimal effort and come with easy-to-clean tracks that also drain water. Available with multi point lock options and various motorised modes, the door can be customised based on your choice of colour and design.   

16 SERIES

Our doors just got sleeker. With a profile wall thickness of just 16 mm, this door is great for internal partitions and also functions as a privacy partition with biometric lock options. The doors are trackless and can be offered in a range of glass options that are provided exclusively by The Schon. These doors are available in sliding, synchronised and telescopic variations.

Bi-FOLD DOORS

This aluminium glass door can be folded not once but multiple times. An ideal solution for when you want a lovely panoramic view of your beautiful surroundings. Not just stylish but extremely sturdy as well, these doors can be opened based on your need and convenience.

SOLID DOORS

These solid entry doors are highly aesthetic and come with the promise of incomparable quality and high performance when it comes to functionality and most importantly security. Each solid door is built while keeping modern and minimalistic design as the most important design element. The fascia of these doors are available in HPL, stone lam and veneer. The doors can also be customised and made as forward flush doors, in-line doors, hidden doors and frameless doors.
