SUNROOMS

SUNROOM - SH001

When you want to get close to nature, but are afraid of being burned by the hot summer sun, when you want to go outside from home to breathe fresh air and expand your romantic life outdoors a little, choose a suitable Schon sunroom for your home, and your desire to enjoy nature, bask in the sun and look at the stars can be infinitely satisfied.

Sunroom product Superiority

With SC6063-S5 Aviation Aluminum
After adopting high-strength aluminum alloy profiles, the structural layer and decorative layer combine perfectly.
Can be used with panoramic skylight or electric skylight.
Providing various types of glass roofs such as tempered, laminated, hollow glass.
A highly closed process structure ensures sealing performance.
It is a new and beautiful shape, safe construction.
