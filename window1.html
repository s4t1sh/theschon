WINDOWS

German Engineered for Indian Homes

Need windows that do more than just open and close? You've come to the right place! Schon brings you Aluminium glass windows that are not just good to look at but come with a bunch of extra perks. Our sleek powder-coated aluminium sliding windows are designed to keep out noise, keep in warmth, seal out water, and stand strong against the wind.

Every window we make is crafted to handle the ups and downs of Indian weather and customized to match your style. They add a touch of luxury and sophistication to your home. Plus, we haven't forgotten about convenience — our windows are super easy to clean, making your life a little easier.

Choose from our wide range of window solutions like aluminium sliding windows perfect for your living room, stylish casement windows for an unobstructed view, anodized aluminium windows that resist the elements, or aluminium windows specially designed for your kitchen and balcony.

Pick Schon for windows that blend in function, comfort, and elegance, all tailored to your needs.

Distinctive Features | The Schon Window Advantage

  • Smooth Operation: Our windows offer unparalleled ease of use with a glide that feels effortless, featuring innovative sliding mechanisms.
  • Robust Build: Each aluminium glass window boasts a substantial 1.6 mm thickness, ensuring a solid, sturdy construction.
  • Versatile Designs: Tailor your experience with our single or double track options, providing flexibility to fit any design aesthetic.
  • Durability: Engineered for endurance, our windows are designed with superior load-bearing capacity to withstand the test of time.
  • Custom Aesthetics: Choose from a diverse palette of colours with our powder-coated aluminium windows, crafted to complement your home's unique style.

Ultra-Slim Sliding Windows

Our ultra-slim sliding windows, ranging from 1.6 mm to 5.00 mm in profile wall thickness, are available in both single and double track options. They come equipped with an anti-collision strip, ensuring enhanced security and noise reduction. Boasting high-load endurance and long-lasting performance, these sleek windows merge safety with elegance.

Bi-Fold Windows

Our bi-fold aluminum glass windows offer a flexible, stylish way to enjoy uninterrupted views of your surroundings. Designed to fold multiple times, they provide a broad, clear vista when you want it and fold neatly away when you don't. These sturdy and fashionable windows adjust to your needs while maximizing your space and view.

Casement Windows

The aluminum casement windows at The Schön feature a systemized design complete with insulating tempered glass and top-notch international hardware. Offering both tilt and turn options, these windows are also fitted with flyscreens for an insect-free environment, ensuring comfort and convenience without compromising on style.

Curtain Wall Windows

For a dramatic effect and a flood of natural light, our curtain wall windows consist of multiple glass panels set within a secure frame. This design is mounted on a building's exterior, creating an illusion of a glass façade that minimizes visible profiles and maximizes your view and daylight exposure.
