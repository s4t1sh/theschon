About Us

Welcome to Schon — where your quest for luxury and practicality in home living finds its answer. Our name means 'beautiful' in German, a standard we uphold in every custom-designed window, door, wardrobe, and kitchen we create. Schon is about bringing your vision to life with a touch of elegance, from chic glass features to contemporary metalwork.
Quality and craftsmanship are at the heart of Schon's ethos. We offer personalized, engineered solutions to make your space truly yours. Our team works hand-in-hand with you to ensure your home reflects your personal style, down to the finest detail.
Choose Schon for a seamless experience that prioritizes your satisfaction. We're not just about transforming spaces; we're about perfecting your home with simplicity and style. Trust Schön to be your partner in crafting a home that's as beautiful as it is functional.

Our Commitment

HOW IT WORKS ?

Want to make your dream home a reality? Here’s how to start with Schon:

  1. Get in Touch: Just give us a call or drop us a message.
  2. Let’s Chat: We’d love to meet you! Come have a coffee with us at our showroom to talk about your ideas.
  3. See the Possibilities: We’ll show you around and give you a taste of how we can make your space look and feel amazing.
  4. Tailored for You: Our designers will talk with you to understand what you want, visit your home to get a better idea, and then draw up designs just for you.
  5. Your Approval: You check and give us the thumbs up on the design.
  6. We Build: Then we take those designs and turn them into reality.
  7. Installation: In just 90 days, we’ll install everything and make sure it's all done perfectly.
